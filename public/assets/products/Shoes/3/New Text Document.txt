The Iconic Shoe that Brought our Sidestripe to Life: This is the Old Skool. First unveiled back in 1977, the Vans Old Skool has been near the top of the bestseller list ever since thanks to its classic fit and instantly recognizable design. This black and navy version takes the Classic look and brings up a notch, allowing the twin shimmer of black and navy to breathe new life into the shoe. Reinforced toe caps, supportive padded colors, and the signature rubber waffle outsoles take care of the practical side, while the color speaks for itself. The Old Skool Primary is the obvious choice for anyone looking to bridge the divide between iconic and innovative.
